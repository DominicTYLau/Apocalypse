Sacrifices

Welcome to Apocalypse. Sacrifice life points to win.

Start with 20 life points and if it gets to 0 you lose!

Sacrificing

Turn cards horizontally to sacrifice them

Some card will have special abilities that will come in play when sacrificed

Resurrecting

You will have ato pay a certain amount of life points in order to resurrect a card

All cards can be sacrificed and resurrected infintiely as long as you have health points and it is your turn to "set up"

Attacking

You can send any amount of cards into attack, if the attack points of your card is greater than the health points of the card being attacked, the card will, die, but the opposing cards attack damage will be dealt to your card
